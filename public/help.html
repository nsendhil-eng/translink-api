App Features & Guide

Searching for Stops & Routes

The main search bar is your starting point. You can search for:

  • Stops: Type a stop name (e.g., "King George Square") or stop code.
  • Routes: Type a route number (e.g., "444" or "P331").
  • Suburbs: Type a suburb name to see routes that service that area.

As you type, a list of suggestions will appear. Click on an item to interact with it.

Selecting & Managing Stops

  • Add/Remove: Click a stop from the search results to add it to your selection. Clicking it again will remove it. Selected stops appear as tags above the map.
  • Parent Stations: Some stops (like busway or train stations) are grouped. Click the `+` icon to expand and see all the individual platforms.
  • Clear All: Use the "Clear All" button that appears next to the tags to remove all selected stops at once.

Using the Interactive Map

  • Stop Markers: Selected stops are automatically plotted on the map. Click a marker to see its name and get walking directions.

  • Filtered Routes: When you filter by one or more routes in the departures list, their paths will be drawn on the map for easy comparison.
  • Walking Directions: Click the icon next to any stop name (in the search results or on a map popup) to see a walking route from your current location.

Viewing & Filtering Departures

  • Live Times: The departure list on the right shows live upcoming services for your selected stops. It automatically refreshes every 10 seconds.
  • Route Filters: Once stops are selected, a list of servicing routes appears above the departures. Click on one or more route numbers to filter the list and see only the services you're interested in.
  • Mobile Filters: On mobile, tap the "Filter Routes" button to open a multi-select dropdown, saving screen space while providing the same functionality.
